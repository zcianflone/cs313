Welcome to Me

I love working with computers and programming, which is why I'm pursuing this degree online. I find myself unable to shake an interest in tech that I've had since childhood.

I also enjoy writing. I double majored in English and Philosophy at BYU and got a Master's in Fiction Writing at Brooklyn College. I'm writing a novel based on my dad's experience as a roadie for the funk band Parliament-Funkadelic in the 70s.

While I was attending grad school, I got a job at the Apple Store as a Mac Genius. When I graduated, I realized that I'd also graduated with a deepened loved of computing. I want to get a little closer to understanding how computers really work.

I also like to ski and I've got a burgeoning interest in cooking. My wife and I love to travel.
